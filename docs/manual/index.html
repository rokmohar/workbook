What is Workbook?

WorkBook is a social-network web application for connecting with your co-workers, family memebrs and friends.

You create your own profile. You can search for people you know and you can connect with them by sending them a friend request. They have to accept your request and then they are added to your friends list. You can organize them into circles for connecting with them easier and faster.

You can add different kind of posts to your profile, from text messages, photos, external content (e.g. blogs, web sites, youtube videos, etc.). You can set privacy for each post and limit its visibility only to selected group of people (e.g. everyone, selected circles, selected individuals).

You can react to and comment posts from people you are connected with.

How to start

You can start using it by first accessing the account registration page. You can access the registration page by clicking the button on login page, which loads when you first enter our website URL into your browser address bar, which loads when you access our website for the first time and you must not be logged in to see it.

If you are logged in to some different account you must first logout.

How to register

You can register by filling out data in the registration form. You are required to enter your name, email address and chosee your own secret password.

A confirmation email will be sent to you, which contains a link that is used to confirm your email address. You cannot access your account without doing this.

How to add friends

You can find your friends and acquaintances by searching for them by name. On their profile you will find add friend button. By clicking this button you will send them a friend request, which they will have to accept, before you become their friend.

How to post

You can post content by filling out the post form on homepage or your profile page. You can post raw text, external image or youtube video.

You can set privacy to your post. By doing this you will tell our application to whom it should display this post to. It could be either public, friends only or private - visible only to you.

You can later edit your post by clicking the edit button, where you can change the content, post type or privacy.

How to like

If you like something that you see, you can like it. You can do this by clicking the like button under any post that you can see.

This is done without refreshing the page and the like counter is dynamically updated, after you like was accepted by the server.

If you don't like something that you have liked, you can still unlike it, by clicking the unlike button, which appears when you have liked something.

How to comment

If you want to comment something, you can do this by submitting the form below any post, which you have access to.

 

You can later edit your post, if you want to change something, or if you made a spelling mistake.
